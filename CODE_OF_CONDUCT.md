# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this portfolio project respectful, constructive, and welcoming for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Keep design and engineering feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect the privacy and attribution of people, organizations, and research collaborators represented by the site.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Sharing credentials, private correspondence, or restricted project material.
- Submitting copied work without attribution.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block participants whose behavior harms the project community.
